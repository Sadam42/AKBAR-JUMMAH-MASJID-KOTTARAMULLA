Welcome to Our Masjid

A Place of Worship & Community

Akbar Jummah Masjid stands as a beacon of faith and community in Kottaramulla, strategically located in front of People's Bank on the main road towards Natthandiya from Dankotuwa.

Our masjid is designed to provide a welcoming, clean, and peaceful environment for all worshippers, regardless of their background or experience level.

With modern amenities and a commitment to excellence in every aspect, we strive to create a spiritual sanctuary where families and individuals can connect with their faith.
